Data Exploration and Visualization:

Perform an in-depth analysis of the dataset to uncover trends, correlations, and anomalies.
Visualize key metrics such as average house prices, distribution by neighborhoods, and the influence of property features (e.g., number of bedrooms, square footage) on pricing.
Insight Development:

Generate meaningful insights that reveal critical factors affecting house prices.
Identify high-demand areas and property types, and highlight discrepancies or opportunities for pricing adjustments.
Interactive Storyboard Creation:

Develop at least five dashboards (excluding the summary and insights dashboards) to present various aspects of the data.
Ensure the dashboards are interactive, visually appealing, and easy to interpret, following a consistent color scheme and minimal clutter.
Summary and Recommendations Dashboard:

Create a comprehensive summary dashboard to encapsulate key insights and provide actionable recommendations.
This dashboard should offer a quick overview for Senior Management to facilitate data-driven decision-making.
Final Presentation to Senior Management:

Present a cohesive narrative through the storyboard, illustrating how your analysis supports the improvement of the House Recommendation System.
Highlight actionable steps, such as focusing on specific neighborhoods or property types, to enhance customer targeting and optimize pricing strategies.
